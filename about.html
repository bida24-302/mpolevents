Founded

From Passion to Profession

Mpole's Events began as a small home-based catering service in Gaborone, founded by Debbie Tsholofelo Mpole. What started as a passion for creating beautiful events for friends and family quickly grew into a full-fledged business as word spread about our exceptional food and attention to detail.

In 2012, we expanded our services to include event decoration, recognizing that the complete event experience required both culinary excellence and stunning visual design. This holistic approach set us apart and became our signature offering.

Today, with over 500 events under our belt, we've grown into one of Botswana's most trusted event planning companies, known for our creativity, reliability, and personalized service.

Our Commitment

At Mpole's Events, we're committed to more than just beautiful events. We believe in sustainable practices that honor our community and environment.

  • Sourcing 80% of our ingredients from local farmers and producers
  • Using biodegradable and reusable decor materials whenever possible
  • Donating excess food to local shelters and food banks
  • Investing in training programs for young Batswana in hospitality
